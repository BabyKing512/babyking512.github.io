Today' exercise is about the calculation of integral.

Exercise

Calculate $$ \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{\, dx}{(1+x^{2})(1+x^{2019})}$$

Proof

Proof: $$\begin{align} & \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{\, dx}{(1+x^{2})(1+x^{2019})} \\ = & \int_{0}^{1} \frac{\, dx}{(1+x^{2})(1+x^{2019})} + \int_{1}^{\infty} \frac{\, dx}{(1+x^{2})(1+x^{2019})} \\ = & \int_{0}^{1} \frac{\, dx}{(1+x^{2})(1+x^{2019})} + \int_{0}^{1} \frac{x^{2019}\, dx}{(1+x^{2})(1+x^{2019})} \\ & = \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{\, dx}{(1+x^{2})} \\ = & \frac{\pi}{2} \end{align}$$
